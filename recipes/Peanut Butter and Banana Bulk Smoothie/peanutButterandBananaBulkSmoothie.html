Peanut Butter and Banana Bulk Smoothie

Description

This smoothie is perfect to add if you're looking to bulk up or build more muscle, especially those with a fast metabolism! This easy recipe is so simple just add one full banana and 2 scoops of peanut butter.


Ingredients

  • 2 scoops of protein
  • 1 banana
  • 2 Tbsp of peanut butter
  • 12 oz of milk
  • Ice optional

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend and serve.
